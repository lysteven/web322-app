About

Hello, I’m Steven Ly. I’m currently studying Computer Programming & Analysis at Seneca Polytechnic, where I’m focused on building my skills in software development and cloud computing.

In my career so far, I’ve had the chance to work across a few different industries—healthcare, banking, and IT. I’ve interned at companies like IBM, RBC, and CIBC, where I got hands-on experience with tools like Azure, Kubernetes, Linux, and automation scripting with Bash and PowerShell. It’s been exciting to be part of teams solving real-world problems and building scalable systems. Right now, I’m working towards growing into a cloud solutions architect or DevOps engineer. I’m eager to learn as much as I can, keep improving, and eventually gain a deeper understanding of new emerging technologies in my professional career. When I’m not working on my professional goals, I enjoy PC gaming, and I like to unwind by playing golf or badminton.
